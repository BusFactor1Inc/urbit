    1. copy watt/X.watt to watt/Y.watt

    3. update stage number in watt/Y.watt
    4. cp -r genX to genY
    5. git add genY/*.c genY/*.h genY/*/*.c genY/*/*.y
    6. update genY/pit.h and genY/6/ream.y
    7. add genY/ofiles in Makefile
    8. link jets in c/eyre.c
    9. update FirstKernel in c/eyre.c (if desired); check in FK.pile
    10. basic low-grade test of new kernel:
